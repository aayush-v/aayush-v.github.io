Aayush Atul Verma

I'm an incoming PhD student at the ASU Active Perception Group led by Dr. Yezhou Yang. My research interests focus on computer vision, particularly neuromorphic vision. I aim to delve into the temporal aspects of this modality and explore how it can be integrated with other modalities for enhanced perception and understanding.

I earned my Master's degree in Computer Science from Arizona State University in 2024, following my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani) in 2021. Prior to starting my Master's, I spent a year working at Postman, where I gained valuable industry experience.
